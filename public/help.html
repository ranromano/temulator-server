Welcome to Teamulator!

Teamulator lets you divide your friends into fair sports groups according to their rank.

First sign in to the app by choosing a username and a password.

Next, add and optionally rank your friends (you can find them by their username)

After adding all you friends, you can start dividing into groups. Mark all the relevant friends and press Teamulate! You will be redirected to the 'Teams' tab to see who's against who!

You can also edit a player by swiping right and choosing edit player. There you can re-rank a player or completely remove him from your friends list.

General notes regarding the project:

The web application was built using the ionicframework, an open-source front-end SDK for developing advanced cross platform HTML5 mobile apps. Ionic is based on angular.js, making it familiar to a large javascript userbase.

Persistency is achieved via a MongoDB-as-a-Service named Mongolab , that acts as our user repository.

Have fun!
